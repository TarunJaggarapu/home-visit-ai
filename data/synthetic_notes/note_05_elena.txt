Home Visit Note — Participant ID: HV-005
Date of visit: 03/18

Participant is an 84-year-old woman living with her adult son. She has arthritis and mild vision loss. Her son helps with medications but works evenings, so she is alone most nights. She reported one near-fall last week when getting up at night, attributing it to poor lighting in the hallway. She takes five medications and admitted she is sometimes unsure whether she has taken her evening dose. She was oriented and conversational with no memory concerns beyond occasional word-finding difficulty. The bathroom has a grab bar by the toilet but not in the shower. She expressed that she feels safe and supported overall but worries about falling when no one is home.
