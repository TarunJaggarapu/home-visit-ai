Home Visit Note — Participant ID: HV-010
Date of visit: 03/25

Participant is a 90-year-old man living alone in a rural farmhouse. He is fiercely independent and resistant to accepting help. He has had three falls in the past three months, one resulting in a brief hospitalization. He takes several heart medications but was vague about the schedule and could not produce the bottles when asked. He showed some short-term memory difficulty. High fall risk, questionable medication adherence, and limited caregiver support make this a high-priority case.
